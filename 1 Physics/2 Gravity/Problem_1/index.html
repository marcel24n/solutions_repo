Cancel \( m \) and solve for orbital velocity \( v \):

\[ v^2 = \frac{G M}{r} \]

\[ \left(\frac{2\pi r}{T}\right)^2 = \frac{G M}{r} \]
\[ \frac{4\pi^2 r^2}{T^2} = \frac{G M}{r} \]

Multiply both sides by \( T^2 r \):

\[ 4\pi^2 r^3 = G M T^2 \]

Now solve for \( T^2 \):

\[ \boxed{T^2 = \frac{4\pi^2}{G M} \cdot r^3} \]
